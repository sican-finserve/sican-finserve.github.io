Board of Directors

Mr. Ashwani Kumar


Mr. Ashwani Kumar has over 18 years of industry experience, especially in microfinance and Priority Sector domains. Currently, he is the Co-Founder & Managing Director at SiCAN FinServe Private Limited.
He was the Managing Director & CEO at Utkarsh CoreInvest Limited before founding Sican. He has handled senior management roles in different control and support functions at Utkarsh ever since its NBFC-MFI format viz. Utkarsh Micro Finance Pvt. Ltd. and also in its Small Finance Bank format viz. Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Ltd. He had exposure in different capacities, starting as Head Internal Audit, with the concurrent charge of Risk, Compliance, Secretarial, Credit & Client Interaction and then Head Training, Recruitment and Corporate Communication verticals. His last stint with Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Ltd. was as the Deputy CFO (Head Finance and Corporate Communication).
Prior to joining Utkarsh, he was with NABARD Financial Services Ltd. (NABFINS) at its Bangalore Head Office as AGM (Finance); with Canara Bank at its Bangalore Head Office as Manager in the Priority Credit Wing and with Locus Research and Consultants Pvt. Ltd. at its New Delhi Head Office as a Research Associate.
He is a Rural Management Graduate from IRMA, CFA from ICFAI (India), B. Com (Hons.) from Delhi University; has CAIIB and other Diplomas and Certifications from IIBF and is also UGC Net (Management) certified.



Mr. Indrajit Kumar


Indrajit Kumar, Co-founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO), has 18 years of diversified experience in Microfinance, Banking, Business Development, and Project Management. He has demonstrated outstanding performance in expanding operations and embracing technological innovations. He excels in establishing systems and comprehensive work processes, with expertise in setting up operations from the ground and successfully scaling them.
In his role as the National Business Head at Save Microfinance Ltd., Mr. Kumar played a pivotal role in significantly enhancing the company's performance within short span of time. He spearheaded technological transformation and established tech-enabled backend operations. At Satin Creditcare Network Ltd., as the Business Head (Senior Vice President), Mr. Kumar managed a large portfolio while ensuring excellent portfolio quality.
As AGM (Operations) at NABARD Financial Services Ltd. (NABFINS), he was responsible for designing business strategies, BC empanelment, monitoring existing portfolios, and formulating loan policies. He led the Second Level Institutions (SLI) vertical. At the World Bank’s Bihar Kosi Flood Recovery Project (BKFRP: Micro-Housing), He served as the Chief Programme Coordinator, overseeing the planning and supervision of the project, which covered over 30,000 families across three districts. He started his career as a Manager in Canara Bank Circle Office, Chennai, he was one of the top performing Managers, contributing significantly to the Circle’s Agricultural Finance and Priority Sector lending achievements.
Indrajit is a Management Graduate from the Institute of Rural Management, Anand (IRMA), with diplomas and certifications from the Indian Institute of Banking & Finance (IIBF). He is also UGC NET (Management) certified. He actively participates in various industry and academic conferences. His insightful articles on microfinance have been published in “The Financial Express” and “Outlook Money,” and he has also written about Agri-Business in RBI’s Annual Book.
