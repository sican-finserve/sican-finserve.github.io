Board of Directors

Mr. Ashwani Kumar


BACKGROUND

Co-Promoter and Managing Director (MD). Hands-on BFSI industry experience of over 19 years, especially in the domains of banking & microfinance with a focus on setting & upscaling support & control functions, while working with Public Sector Bank, NBFC-MFI, Small Finance Bank & Core-Investment HoldCo.
PRIOR EXPERIENCE
At Utkarsh Group entities (HO, Varanasi), he led critical functions in its transformational journey (MFI to banking licence to IPO) of Utkarsh Micro Finance Pvt. Ltd. (UMFPL) to Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Ltd. (USFBL). Initially he led the Internal Audit vertical with concurrent charges of Risk, Compliance, Secretarial, Credit & Client Interaction & then was Head of Strategy, Corporate Communication, Training and Recruitment verticals. Lastly, he was the Deputy CFO at USFBL, and then the Managing Director & CEO at Utkarsh CoreInvest Ltd. (UCL). Besides, he has also been on the Board of Utkarsh Welfare Foundation for over a decade.
At NABARD Financial Services Ltd. (NABFINS) (HO, Bangalore), as Assistant General Manager, during its transformation journey, he played a pivotal role in setting up and expanding the Finance & Accounts vertical along with the Risk, Audit, Inspection, Secretarial, Regulatory Compliances, HR & General Administration verticals; while being a Business Partner Empanelment Committee Member.
At Canara Bank (HO, Bangalore) as Manager (Scale II), he led the Bank’s 1st Batch of Agri Business Marketing Managers & Officers, concurrently heading the Priority Small Loan Monitoring Division and being part of the initial team to set up the first Microfinance Division of the Bank, which subsequently grew to be a separate ‘Financial Inclusion Wing’ at the Head Office. He also played a pivot role for the Capacity Building initiatives in the Priority & Hi-Tech Agriculture domains, fostering career of the Agriculture Managers & Officers at Bank’s College.
He started his career as a ‘Research Associate’ at Locus Research and Consultants Pvt. Ltd. (HO, New Delhi), primarily engaged in the Projects for Ministry of Rural Development, Govt. of India and other few other international agencies.
An IRMA Graduate, CFA (ICFAI, India), B.Com (Hons.) (Delhi University), CAIIB & various Diplomas & Certifications (IIBF), UGC Net (Mgmt.)
He has been on Editorial Board of institutional magazines and invited at various industry and academic workshops & conferences, as Guest Speaker, Jury/Panel Member. His award wining presentation & article on Channel Financing was published in RBI’s Annual Book.



Mr. Indrajit Kumar


Indrajit Kumar, Co-founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO), has 18 years of diversified experience in Microfinance, Banking, Business Development, and Project Management. He has demonstrated outstanding performance in expanding operations and embracing technological innovations. He excels in establishing systems and comprehensive work processes, with expertise in setting up operations from the ground and successfully scaling them.
In his role as the National Business Head at Save Microfinance Ltd., Mr. Kumar played a pivotal role in significantly enhancing the company's performance within short span of time. He spearheaded technological transformation and established tech-enabled backend operations. At Satin Creditcare Network Ltd., as the Business Head (Senior Vice President), Mr. Kumar managed a large portfolio while ensuring excellent portfolio quality.
As AGM (Operations) at NABARD Financial Services Ltd. (NABFINS), he was responsible for designing business strategies, BC empanelment, monitoring existing portfolios, and formulating loan policies. He led the Second Level Institutions (SLI) vertical. At the World Bank’s Bihar Kosi Flood Recovery Project (BKFRP: Micro-Housing), He served as the Chief Programme Coordinator, overseeing the planning and supervision of the project, which covered over 30,000 families across three districts. He started his career as a Manager in Canara Bank Circle Office, Chennai, he was one of the top performing Managers, contributing significantly to the Circle’s Agricultural Finance and Priority Sector lending achievements.
Indrajit is a Management Graduate from the Institute of Rural Management, Anand (IRMA), with diplomas and certifications from the Indian Institute of Banking & Finance (IIBF). He is also UGC NET (Management) certified. He actively participates in various industry and academic conferences. His insightful articles on microfinance have been published in “The Financial Express” and “Outlook Money,” and he has also written about Agri-Business in RBI’s Annual Book.
