Advisor

Mr. Suyash Rai


Suyash Rai is a Fellow and Deputy Director at Carnegie Endowment for International Peace India. His present research interests are in the areas of political economy, public institutions, and professions in India. Prior to joining the Carnegie Endowment, he was a Fellow at the National Institute of Public Finance and Policy.
He has also previously worked at ICICI Bank and Dvara Trust. He has held consulting positions at the Consultative Group to Assist the Poor (CGAP) of the World Bank and the Ministry of Finance, Government of India. His writings have appeared in publications such as the IMF Working Paper Series, the ADB Working Paper Series, the Wall Street Journal, Seminar Magazine, and Indian Express. He has also contributed papers in several edited volumes.

Dr. Varuni Yadav


Dr. Varuni Yadav possesses a diverse and accomplished professional background with over a decade of experience in Human Resources, Education, Editing, and Law. Her extensive expertise has been instrumental in aligning HR and legal strategies with organizational objectives, with a focus on department growth and development to achieve optimal outcomes.
Dr. Yadav has significant experience in teaching and training, having successfully instructed and mentored over 5,000 students and employees, thereby enhancing productivity and performance across various sectors. As the Editor-in-Chief of the Law Times Journal, she led a prominent online legal education platform dedicated to providing free legal resources to students and professionals.
Her academic contributions include her role as an Assistant Professor at AMITY University and other distinguished institutions. Dr. Yadav has authored 12 publications, further cementing her reputation as a thought leader in her fields of expertise.
In the legal domain, her skills encompass policy review, contract drafting, and litigation management, supported by her membership with the Bar Council. Her human resources expertise includes training and development, recruitment, compliance audits, and strategic planning, showcasing her strong competencies in both HR and legal disciplines.
Dr. Yadav holds a Ph.D. from GGS Indraprastha University, Delhi, an MBA from IMT Ghaziabad, and an LLB from Delhi University. Additionally, she is UGC NET Certified and completed her graduation from Hindu College, Delhi University.

Ms. Prerna Sharma


Prerna Sharma is a seasoned consultant with over a decade of experience in environmental sustainability, biodiversity conservation, and ESG training. Her expertise lies in integrating biodiversity enhancement with sustainable development goals (SDGs), and she has played a pivotal role in guiding both corporate and academic stakeholders towards measurable ecological impact.
Throughout her career, Prerna has engaged with a diverse range of organizations and initiatives. She has led ESG training programs for global corporates such as AISAN and Fortum, and spearheaded biodiversity assessments under the “Model Eco Village Project” with Hindustan Unilever and Parmarth. As an adjunct faculty at Shri Vishwakarma Skill University and a visiting faculty at KKMII Stratford University, she has mentored thousands of students in sustainability and environmental sciences. Her work as a LINC Fellow and editorial contributor to Samvad Broadcast further highlights her commitment to knowledge dissemination and public engagement. Additionally, her contributions as a freelance consultant and technical assistant at the University of Delhi underline her versatility in both applied and research-driven roles.
She holds an MSc in Biotechnology and a BSc in Botany, Chemistry & Zoology from Maharshi Dayanand University. She is currently pursuing a PhD in Phytotechnology with a focus on ecosystem services and climate resilience. She has further enhanced her professional credentials with a Postgraduate Diploma in Bioinformatics and multiple certifications, including Professional Green Buildings (GEM), Master Trainer in Agriculture, and a CSIR-CIMAP program on medicinal plants. Her academic and professional journey is backed by numerous publications, symposium presentations, and a strong foundation in sustainability tools and research methodologies.


Ms. Yachna Srivastava


Yachna Srivastava is a Senior Development Consultant with expertise in the domains of Gender, Health, Livelihoods & Monitoring. As a development professional, she come with over 20 years of experience leading impactful programs across gender equality, maternal and child health, adolescent well-being, education, and sustainable livelihoods. She brings a rare blend of grassroots sensitivity and strategic leadership, having worked with organizations like PATH, CARE, PRADAN, Digital Green, GIZ, and ActionAid across Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, and other regions of India.
Yachna specializes in Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E), baseline and endline studies, randomized control trials (RCTs), and policy-relevant research. Her work is rooted in community engagement—especially with women, adolescent girls, and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)—and is driven by a strong belief in inclusive and data-informed development.
She has supported institutional capacity building, led skill and labor market assessments, and designed frameworks for gender-responsive programming. Yachna continues to advise civil society organizations, government programs, and donors, ensuring that development initiatives are not only well-designed—but also measurable, meaningful, and equitable.
With a postgraduate degree in Social Work from TISS and a recent specialization in Data Science and Business Analytics from the University of Texas, Yachna combines deep field knowledge with strong technical capabilities in tools like SPSS, Python, Tableau, and Atlas.ti.
